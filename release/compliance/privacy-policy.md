## English

### 1. What Little Mandarin Classics is

Little Mandarin Classics / 小小中文经典 is a Chinese classic story reading app for overseas Chinese and bilingual children ages 5-8 and their parents. The app provides daily 5-8 minute child-safe Chinese story reading, pinyin, audio/TTS, vocabulary with English meanings, three comprehension questions, progress, a parent report, and controlled AI explanations limited to the current story.

The first stories are child-safe adaptations of Three Kingdoms content. The app is designed around data minimization, child safety, and parent visibility.

### 2. Children privacy commitments

For the MVP baseline, we do not require or knowingly collect a child's real name, email address, phone number, birthday, school, grade, photo, contacts, precise location, advertising ID, or other direct child identity information.

We do not provide open chat, social posting, public profiles, direct messaging, behavioral advertising, third-party ads, or unrestricted web access for children. AI explanations are limited to the current story and are not an open conversation.

If a future feature would collect personal information from a child, it must pass a pre-launch COPPA/GDPR-K review, update this policy, and obtain verifiable parental consent or parental authorization where required.

### 3. Information we collect or process

#### Local reading progress

The app may store reading progress on the device, such as stories opened or completed, quiz results, reading days, words reviewed, pinyin settings, text size, and parent-report summaries. This information is used to provide the reading experience and parent report. The MVP design keeps this progress local unless a reviewed account or sync feature is added later.

#### Anonymous product analytics

If analytics are enabled, we may collect anonymous app interaction events such as `app_open`, `story_open`, `paragraph_audio_play`, `pinyin_toggle`, `vocab_open`, `quiz_start`, `quiz_complete`, `ai_explain_request`, `story_complete`, and `parent_report_open`.

Analytics must not include a child's name, parent email, free-form child text, precise location, contacts, photos, advertising ID, Android ID, IDFA, or other device identifiers. Analytics are used to understand whether families can complete stories and where the reading experience needs improvement. They are not used for advertising or cross-app tracking.

#### Controlled AI explanations

If the AI explanation feature is enabled through a backend, the app may send only the information needed to answer a current-story question, such as story ID, selected word or sentence, question type, and a coarse age band. The AI must answer only about the current story, with short child-appropriate explanations, and must refuse off-topic requests.

The MVP disclosure assumes no persistent child profile and no retained prompt/response history. Any AI backend logging, retention, vendor use, model training use, or additional fields must pass privacy and child-safety review before launch and be reflected in this policy and store disclosures.

#### Parent feedback

The MVP may include an optional parent feedback form. Feedback may include satisfaction rating, child age band, issue type, suggestion text, and an optional parent contact email. Parent contact information is optional, is treated as parent data, and is used only to respond to the feedback or support request. Children should not enter contact information or personal details.

#### Future parent account

If accounts are added, the account must be parent-oriented. Any parent email, authentication data, payment data, cloud sync, or deletion workflow must be reviewed before launch and disclosed in this policy and store privacy forms. Child profiles must remain optional and non-identifying.

### 4. Information we do not collect in the MVP baseline

We do not collect child real names, child email addresses, child phone numbers, birthdays, school names, grade/class identifiers, photos, contacts, precise location, microphone recordings, camera data, advertising identifiers, third-party ad data, or open-chat transcripts.

We do not sell personal information. We do not share child personal information for advertising or tracking.

### 5. How we use information

We use the limited information above to:

- Provide story reading, pinyin, audio/TTS, vocabulary, quizzes, progress, and parent reports.
- Remember local settings such as language, pinyin default, text size, and audio settings.
- Improve app reliability and reading completion through anonymous analytics.
- Respond to optional parent feedback.
- Operate controlled AI explanations if enabled.

### 6. Sharing and service providers

We do not sell user data and do not use third-party advertising SDKs.

If service providers are used for analytics, hosting, feedback handling, crash reporting, or AI processing, they must be reviewed before launch for child-directed service suitability, data minimization, security, retention, and prohibition on advertising/tracking use. Store disclosures must include any data collected or shared by those SDKs or services.

For child-directed distribution, the app must not transmit AAID, IDFA, Android ID, precise location, or other device identifiers to third parties.

### 7. Parent choices, deletion, and contact

Parents can remove local progress by deleting the app or using any future in-app reset control. Optional parent feedback contact data can be deleted on request once the operator contact channel is finalized.

If a parent believes a child has provided personal information, the parent should contact the operator so the information can be reviewed and deleted where required.

### 8. Legal and store compliance notes

COPPA gives parents control over information collected from children by covered websites and apps, including requirements for COPPA-compliant procedures and verifiable parental consent when children's personal information is collected. GDPR Article 8 requires parental authorization/consent for children below the applicable member-state age threshold for information society services where consent is the lawful basis. Google Play and Apple require accurate, current privacy disclosures, including third-party SDK practices.

This policy must be updated before release if the app adds accounts, cloud sync, AI prompt logging, analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, payments, subscriptions, external links, parent feedback storage, or any collection of child personal information.

## 中文

### 1. 小小中文经典是什么

Little Mandarin Classics / 小小中文经典是一款面向海外 5-8 岁华人/双语儿童及其家长的中文经典故事阅读 App。App 提供每天 5-8 分钟的儿童安全中文故事阅读、拼音、朗读/TTS、生字英文释义、3 道理解题、阅读进度、家长报告，以及仅围绕当前故事的受控 AI 解释。

首批内容为儿童安全改写的《三国演义》故事。产品设计原则是数据最小化、儿童安全和家长可见。

### 2. 儿童隐私承诺

MVP 基线版本不要求、也不会有意收集儿童真实姓名、儿童邮箱、电话号码、生日、学校、年级、照片、通讯录、精确位置、广告 ID 或其他可直接识别儿童身份的信息。

App 不提供开放聊天、社交发布、公开主页、私信、行为广告、第三方广告或儿童可自由访问的网页。AI 解释只围绕当前故事，不是开放聊天。

如果未来功能需要收集儿童个人信息，必须在上线前完成 COPPA/GDPR-K 合规审查，更新本政策，并在法律要求时取得可验证的家长同意或授权。

### 3. 我们收集或处理的信息

#### 本地阅读进度

App 可能在设备本地保存阅读进度，例如打开或完成的故事、答题结果、阅读天数、复习过的生字、拼音设置、字号设置和家长报告汇总。此类信息用于提供阅读体验和家长报告。MVP 设计默认将进度保存在本地，除非未来新增并经过审查的账号或同步功能。

#### 匿名产品分析

如启用埋点分析，我们可能收集匿名 App 交互事件，例如 `app_open`、`story_open`、`paragraph_audio_play`、`pinyin_toggle`、`vocab_open`、`quiz_start`、`quiz_complete`、`ai_explain_request`、`story_complete`、`parent_report_open`。

埋点不得包含儿童姓名、家长邮箱、儿童自由输入文本、精确位置、通讯录、照片、广告 ID、Android ID、IDFA 或其他设备标识符。埋点仅用于了解家庭是否能顺利完成阅读，以及改进阅读体验；不用于广告或跨 App 追踪。

#### 受控 AI 解释

如启用后端 AI 解释功能，App 只会发送回答当前故事问题所需的最少信息，例如故事 ID、被选中的字词或句子、问题类型和粗略年龄段。AI 只能回答当前故事相关问题，答案需简短、适龄，并拒绝离题请求。

MVP 披露假设不建立持久儿童画像，也不保留儿童提示词/回答历史。任何 AI 后端日志、留存、供应商处理、模型训练用途或额外字段，都必须在上线前完成隐私和儿童安全审查，并同步更新本政策和商店披露。

#### 家长反馈

MVP 可能包含可选的家长反馈表单。反馈可包括满意度、儿童年龄段、问题类型、建议文本，以及可选的家长联系方式。家长联系方式是可选项，属于家长数据，仅用于回复反馈或支持请求。儿童不应输入联系方式或个人信息。

#### 未来家长账号

如果未来新增账号，账号必须面向家长。任何家长邮箱、认证数据、支付数据、云同步或删除流程，都必须在上线前完成审查，并在本政策和商店隐私表单中披露。儿童资料应保持可选且不可识别身份。

### 4. MVP 基线版本不收集的信息

我们不收集儿童真实姓名、儿童邮箱、儿童电话号码、生日、学校名称、年级/班级标识、照片、通讯录、精确位置、麦克风录音、相机数据、广告标识符、第三方广告数据或开放聊天记录。

我们不会出售个人信息，也不会为广告或追踪目的分享儿童个人信息。

### 5. 信息用途

我们使用上述有限信息用于：

- 提供故事阅读、拼音、朗读/TTS、生字、测验、进度和家长报告。
- 记住语言、拼音默认开关、字号和音频等本地设置。
- 通过匿名分析改进 App 可靠性和阅读完成体验。
- 回复可选的家长反馈。
- 在启用时运行受控 AI 解释。

### 6. 共享与服务提供商

我们不出售用户数据，不使用第三方广告 SDK。

如果使用分析、托管、反馈处理、崩溃报告或 AI 处理服务提供商，必须在上线前审查其是否适合儿童导向服务、是否满足数据最小化、安全、留存限制，以及禁止广告/追踪使用。商店披露必须包含这些 SDK 或服务收集或共享的任何数据。

面向儿童分发时，App 不得向第三方传输 AAID、IDFA、Android ID、精确位置或其他设备标识符。

### 7. 家长选择、删除和联系

家长可以通过删除 App 或未来的 App 内重置控制来移除本地进度。可选家长反馈中的联系方式，可在运营方联系方式确定后按请求删除。

如果家长认为儿童提交了个人信息，请联系运营方，以便审查并按要求删除。

### 8. 法律与商店合规说明

COPPA 要求受监管的网站和 App 在收集儿童信息时遵守相应程序，并在收集儿童个人信息时取得可验证的家长同意。GDPR 第 8 条规定，在以同意为法律基础向儿童提供信息社会服务时，低于适用成员国年龄门槛的儿童需要家长责任持有人同意或授权。Google Play 和 Apple 要求隐私披露准确、及时，并覆盖第三方 SDK 实际行为。

如 App 新增账号、云同步、AI 提示词日志、分析 SDK、崩溃报告 SDK、支付、订阅、外部链接、家长反馈存储或任何儿童个人信息收集，本政策必须在发布前更新。
