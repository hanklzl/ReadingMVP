## Claims To Avoid

- Do not say the app is proven, top-rated, endorsed, or loved by families unless real evidence is available and approved.
- Do not promise fluency, test-score improvement, grade improvement, medical/therapeutic outcomes, or guaranteed retention.
- Do not call the AI an "AI friend," "open chatbot," or "answers anything" tutor.
- Do not ask for child names, school names, photos, videos, voice recordings, birthdays, exact location, or direct child contact.
- Do not ask parents to upload child voice; local read-aloud/retell recording, if used, must remain local-only.
- Do not imply any outreach has already been posted, approved, or completed.
