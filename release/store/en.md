# Little Mandarin Classics

## App Name

Little Mandarin Classics

## Subtitle / App Store

Chinese classics for kids

## Short Description / Google Play

Daily Mandarin stories with pinyin, karaoke audio, quizzes, and parent plans.

## Promotional Text

Help your child build a 5-8 minute Mandarin reading habit with child-safe Three Kingdoms stories, pinyin, karaoke read-along, vocabulary review, practice rounds, and a weekly parent action plan.

## Long Description

Little Mandarin Classics is a daily Mandarin reading companion for overseas Chinese and bilingual families with children ages 5-8.

The MVP includes 15 child-safe Three Kingdoms stories adapted in order from public-domain classic source material. Each story is 300-600 Chinese characters, Level 1-3, and designed for one short reading session with pinyin, karaoke read-along audio, 5-8 vocabulary words, 3 comprehension questions, and a retell prompt.

Little Mandarin Classics is not a full Chinese course or a replacement for Chinese school. It is a focused classic-story reading routine that helps children move from "I understand some Chinese" toward "I can read one Chinese story today."

### A Daily Reading Path

- Start with a short onboarding placement check
- Follow an adaptive Today recommendation for the next story and review amount
- Read 15 ordered Three Kingdoms stories at Level 1-3
- Use pinyin support without making every story depend on pinyin forever
- Listen with per-character karaoke highlighting and Qwen child-voice narration
- Tap words in the story for curated meanings, pinyin, and English support

### Story Tools For Children

- Controlled story explanations for selected words, sentences, and questions
- A vocabulary notebook with spaced repetition review
- A next-day review pack for missed questions and due words
- Interactive practice rounds: sentence ordering, word matching, and cloze
- Gentle sound effects for practice feedback
- Read-aloud and retell recording stored locally for parent review
- A story-driven ability map across character recognition, word meaning, sentence reading, listening, comprehension, retelling, and culture

### Parent Support

Parents can see a weekly action-plan report with stories read, words to review, reading-day progress, quiz support, and a suggested next step. The parent share card is PII-free and designed for family updates, not public ranking.

Parents can also view and delete the on-device AI safety log and manage local read-aloud or retell recordings. Child voice recordings are never uploaded.

### Safety And Privacy

Little Mandarin Classics is designed around COPPA / GDPR-K data minimization. Accounts are parent-only. The app does not ask for a child's real name, school, birthday, photo, location, contacts, or child identifiers. Learning progress, the on-device AI safety log, and local recordings stay on the device and are parent-managed. AI support is limited to controlled explanations about the current story.

The app has no child social features, public leaderboards, third-party advertising, or tracking-based advertising.

### Built For Bilingual Families

Many overseas children can understand some spoken Chinese but need help reading Chinese text independently. Little Mandarin Classics combines bilingual UI, English vocabulary meanings, Chinese story text, pinyin, audio, and short practice loops so families can keep Mandarin reading light, safe, and consistent.
