# Seed Family Screening Form

Purpose: screen the first 100 overseas Chinese/bilingual families for the Little Mandarin Classics MVP. The child user should be age 5-8. The form must be completed by a parent or legal guardian only.

Product summary shown at the top of the form:

> Little Mandarin Classics is a child-safe Chinese classic story reading app for overseas Chinese/bilingual children age 5-8. Each day, a child reads one 5-8 minute adapted classic story with pinyin, audio, vocabulary, 3 comprehension questions, and a parent progress report. The first MVP series is a child-safe Three Kingdoms story sequence.

Privacy note shown before any questions:

> Please do not enter a child real name, child contact information, school name, teacher name, photo, birthday, exact address, or any other child personal information. We only collect parent/guardian contact information and parent-reported learning context for MVP testing.

## Invitation Priority Rules

Use these rules internally to select the first 100 families. Do not expose a score to parents.

High-priority fit:

- Parent/guardian consent is complete.
- Child age range includes 5, 6, 7, or 8.
- Family is in a priority market: United States, Canada, Australia, or Singapore.
- City/community matches early outreach clusters: Bay Area, Los Angeles, New York/New Jersey, Seattle, Boston, Toronto, Vancouver, Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, or Singapore.
- Parent reports an active Chinese learning pain point around reading, consistency, pinyin/audio support, story comprehension, or parent time.
- Parent can test for 4 weeks and is willing to give weekly feedback.
- Family can try at least 2 stories in week 1.
- Parent is open to a parent progress report.
- Parent gives a clear willingness-to-pay signal or price-sensitivity answer.
- Android device is available for the first Android-led MVP validation wave.

Lower-priority or waitlist:

- Child is outside age 5-8.
- Parent cannot test in the next 4 weeks.
- Parent is not willing to provide feedback.
- Family mainly needs a full Chinese curriculum, live tutoring, exam prep, or open AI chat.
- Parent is not comfortable with any controlled AI support.

Do not invite:

- Form was not completed by a parent/legal guardian.
- Submission includes child contact information or asks a child to participate directly.
- Submission includes child real name, school name, teacher name, photo, birthday, exact address, or other child PII.
- Submission asks for use cases outside the child-safe story reading scope.
